# TechCorp Parental Leave Policy

## Maternity Leave
- Duration: 16 weeks paid leave
- Eligibility: Employees with 12+ months tenure
- Extension: Up to 8 additional weeks unpaid upon request

## Paternity Leave
- Duration: 4 weeks paid leave
- Eligibility: Employees with 6+ months tenure
- Must be used within 6 months of child's birth

## Adoption Leave
- Same benefits as biological parents
- Documentation required: Official adoption certificate

## Return-to-Work Program
- Flexible schedule available for first 3 months after return
- Lactation room available on Floor 3

## Benefits Continuation
During parental leave:
- Health insurance: Fully covered by company
- Stock vesting: Continues normally
- Annual leave: Accrues at 50% rate
