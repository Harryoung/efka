# TechCorp Employee Handbook

## Leave Policy

### Annual Leave
- Probation period (first 6 months): 5 days
- After probation: 15 days per year
- Carry-over: Maximum 5 days to next year

### Sick Leave
- 10 days per year with medical certificate
- First 3 days: 100% salary
- Days 4-10: 80% salary

### Special Leave
- Marriage: 3 days (must be used within 3 months of registration)
- Parental: See document `parental_leave_policy.md`
- Bereavement: 3 days for immediate family

## Expense Reimbursement

For reimbursement limits and approval workflow, refer to `travel_expense_policy.md`.

Quick reference:
- Meals during business travel: See Finance Policy Section 2.1
- Equipment purchase: Requires manager approval + Finance approval for items > $500
