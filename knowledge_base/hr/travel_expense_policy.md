# TechCorp Finance Policy

## 1. Budget Approval Matrix

| Amount (USD) | Approver | Processing Time |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|
| < $100 | Self-approval | Immediate |
| $100 - $500 | Direct Manager | 1 business day |
| $500 - $5,000 | Department Head | 3 business days |
| $5,000 - $20,000 | VP + Finance | 5 business days |
| > $20,000 | CEO + Board | 10 business days |
